Mohsin Ramzan

Author of "Fractured Nation"

In this comprehensive book, the author explores Pakistan's rich history, from its birth in the 1947 partition to contemporary dilemmas. It delves into pivotal moments, including political assassinations, conflicts like Kargil and the formation of the Taliban, and the influence of the deep state and militant forces. The narrative traces the origins of Pakistan's identity, challenges stereotypes, and emphasizes the importance of learning from history to make informed choices. It covers a wide range of topics, from the Mughal Empire to societal issues, offering a deeper understanding of Pakistan's complex layers and a call to explore, learn, and reflect for positive change.

About Me

A quiet wanderer, forever captivated by the world of books, entranced by the realm of web design, and devoted to the art of storytelling. Books are my sanctuary, granting refuge from the chaos of reality. Designing is my hobby, where I craft digital dreams and orchestrate visual harmonies that resonate with the soul. Writing is my nature, a medium through which I unveil the truths that often go unsaid. Like a meek boy who finds strength in the power of words, solace in the embrace of design, and purpose in the stories woven through existence. With every turn of a page, every pixel perfectly placed, and every word meticulously chosen, I aspire to find beauty in the ordinary and meaning in the mundane.
